NJKpopper migration

2026-05-25

Ten years ago (2016-04-20 to be exact), I started up a blogspot blog to chronicle the explosion of mental activity that resulted from my encounter with Harmogu’s creativity and the reactivation of the “thoughts and dreams” that are one of the themes of this site as well.

The pattern of posts, 57 in 2016, 17 in 2017, 8 in 2018, and 9 in 2019, reflects that Supernova burst of excitement and then its aftermath.

I typically reevaluate all of my content by at least the ten year mark. I had always planned to move away from the NJKpopper identity since it is Google/Alphabet based, and my aversion to that is well documented elsewhere.

So it is all coming down from the original site, which I never really talked about or linked to. Only a few people who might remember my active social media days would even know the NJKpopper identity symbolized by the image in this post, spun up in the heady enthusiasm of my 2013-2017 peak Kpop phase (my song bank is probably mostly identical with a Korean high school boy of that period and unrelatable to anyone outside that window, which you can tell by looking for the Orange Caramel sticker on my laptop in my work-related posts on another site). I’m only carrying a torch for aespa and the SMTOWN lineage at this point (RIP Sulli).

The mind of NJKpopper moved on to Mongolia in 2016, and that’s reflected in these older posts that mash up many concepts on my mind at the time. Maybe I’d never quite be in the same mood to spill my guts over the internet that way again, but I realize rereading them now that they remain the authentic me, and those same aesthetic elements are still important to me, still things that I am always revisiting in my mind and hoping to refine and recombine in more selectively distributed writing.

A long-winded way of saying that I will be migrating and reposting all of those posts to this site, labeled [migrated from NJKpopper]. I will not translate them to Mongolian. The NJKpopper site will be emptied out and then shut down.
